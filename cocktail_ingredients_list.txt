Cosmopolitan
1 ounce vodka
1/2 ounce cointreau
1 teaspoon fresh lime juice
1 1/2 ounces cranberry juice
1 twist lime zest, garnish

Dirty Martini
6 ounce vodka
1 dash dry vermouth
1 ounce brine from olive jar
4 stuffed green olives, garnish

Mai Tai
1 ounce spiced rum
1/2 ounce coconut-flavored rum
1 teaspoon grenadine syrup
3 ounces pineapple juice
2 ounce orange juice
1 cup of ice cubes

Mojito
3 fresh mint leaves
2 teaspoons white sugar
1 ounce rum
1/2 cup ice cubes
1/4 cup carbonated water
1/2 lime, cut into 6 wedges, garnish

Margarita
1 cup limeade 
1/4 cup pineapple juice
1/4 cup orange juice
2 tablespoons tequila
1 tablespoon orange liqueur

Old Fashioned
2 teaspoons simple syrup
1 teaspoon water
2 dashes bitters
1 cup ice cubes
1 ounce bourbon whiskey
1 slice orange, garnish
1 maraschino cherry, garnish
